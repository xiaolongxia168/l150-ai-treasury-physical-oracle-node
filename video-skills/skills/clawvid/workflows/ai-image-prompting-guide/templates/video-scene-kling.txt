VIDEO SCENE TEMPLATE (KLING 3.0 / HIGGSFIELD)
==============================================

Use with: Kling 3.0 on Higgsfield
Best for: Short-form video content, ads, social clips


SINGLE SCENE PROMPT:
--------------------

[CHARACTER DESCRIPTION from reference sheet]

Scene: [LOCATION/SETTING]

Action: [WHAT HAPPENS - be specific about movement]

Camera: [CAMERA MOVEMENT - e.g., slow push-in, tracking shot, static]

Duration: [5-15 seconds]

Audio: [MOOD/MUSIC STYLE - optional]


EXAMPLE - PRODUCT UNBOXING:
---------------------------

Young woman, early 20s, long brown hair, natural makeup (reference attached).

Scene: Cozy bedroom with warm afternoon light. Sitting on bed with white bedding.

Action: Opens a small package excitedly. Pulls out product. Holds it up to camera with genuine surprised smile. Examines it closely.

Camera: Medium shot, slight slow zoom in as she holds up product.

Duration: 12 seconds

Audio: Upbeat, positive lo-fi background


EXAMPLE - MORNING ROUTINE:
--------------------------

Young woman matching reference sheet.

Scene: Modern bathroom, soft morning light through frosted window.

Action: Applies skincare product to face while looking in mirror. Turns to camera with satisfied smile. Shows product bottle.

Camera: Over-shoulder shot transitioning to front-facing.

Duration: 10 seconds

Audio: Calm, fresh morning vibes


MULTI-SHOT EXAMPLE:
-------------------

Shot 1 (3s): Close-up of hands opening product packaging
Shot 2 (4s): Medium shot of woman's excited reaction
Shot 3 (3s): Close-up of product with soft focus background
Shot 4 (5s): Woman applying/using product with satisfied expression

Transitions: Smooth cuts
Overall mood: Authentic, relatable, aspirational


TIPS FOR VIDEO:
---------------
• Attach start frame reference for character
• Be specific about camera movement
• Keep actions simple and clear
• Include transition notes for multi-shot
• Kling 3.0 handles character consistency automatically
